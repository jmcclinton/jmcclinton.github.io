About Me


Hello! I am Jennifer and I want to be your next Graphic Designer. For over 15 years, I have been creating and delivering digital and marketing materials for various clients and industries, ranging from small businesses and visual artists to educational and cultural organizations. As a freelance graphic designer, I enjoy the flexibility and variety of working on different projects and challenges, while maintaining high standards of quality and customer service.
I have a strong background in communication design, Adobe Creative Cloud, Figma and QuarkXpress, which I acquired through my formal education and professional experience. I also keep updating my skills and knowledge by taking online courses and certificates in UI/UX design, Figma, WordPress, Google Analytics, and other relevant technologies and software. My goal is to provide innovative and user-friendly solutions that meet the needs and expectations of my clients and their audiences.
